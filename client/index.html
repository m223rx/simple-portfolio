About Me

Hey! I’m Mohamed Mortadha, a 20-year-old computer science student from Tunisia who’s absolutely in love with coding and creating cool things online. I spend most of my time building web apps, experimenting with new tech, and designing interfaces that actually feel good to use.

I code mainly in Python, JavaScript, and C, and I’ve been diving into frameworks like Flask while running my own Ubuntu servers. Lately, I’ve been getting more curious about cybersecurity, because I like understanding how things work under the hood and how to make them safer.

Outside of coding, you’ll usually find me at the gym, sketching out designs in Photoshop, or writing music. All of that creative energy helps me bring fresh ideas into my projects.

My big dream? To build multiple companies in different industries, ship products that people genuinely enjoy, and keep learning every single day.
